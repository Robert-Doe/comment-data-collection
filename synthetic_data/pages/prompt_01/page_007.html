MATCH REPORT: Man Utd 4-1 Arsenal | Premier League
March 3, 2002 • Old Trafford

United put on a stunning performance at Old Trafford to dismantle Arsenal 4-1. Goals from van Nistelrooy (2), Beckham, and Scholes. Arsenal's sole consolation came from Henry just before half time. Ferguson's side are now 8 points clear at the top. Share your thoughts below.
