Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons of Liberty (PS2)
Review by: Codec_Snake • Published: November 29, 2001

Kojima has delivered a masterwork of confusion, philosophy, and postmodern game design. The bait-and-switch with Raiden infuriated millions of players including myself at first. By the end I understood exactly what Kojima was doing - and it's brilliant. The gameplay is leaps ahead of the original. A generation-defining game.
Score: 9.5 / 10
