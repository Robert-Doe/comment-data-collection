Chicken Piccata with Capers
Submitted by PastaMom • October 2002

Pound chicken breasts thin, dredge in flour, pan-fry in butter and olive oil until golden. Deglaze with white wine and lemon juice, add capers, swirl in cold butter. Serve immediately over linguine. This has been my go-to dinner party recipe for fifteen years.

Tip from a culinary school grad: make sure your pan is HOT before adding the chicken and don't move it for the first 2 minutes. That's the secret to getting that golden crust without the flour burning. Also, use real unsalted butter for the finishing step, not margarine.
