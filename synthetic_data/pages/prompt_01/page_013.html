Sleep Training at 6 Months: Our Experience with Ferber
By SleepyMomJessica • June 4, 2002

After six months of waking up every 90 minutes I finally broke down and tried Ferber's progressive waiting method. Night one was brutal. By night four she slept six hours straight. I know it's controversial but it worked for our family. I'd love to hear your experiences.
