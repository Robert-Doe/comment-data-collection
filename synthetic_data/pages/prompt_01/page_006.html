Hamfest 2003 Trip Report
Posted by K2DFG, Club Secretary • September 4, 2003

Fifteen club members attended the annual Dayton Hamvention this past weekend. The flea market was enormous this year with over 400 vendors. I personally picked up a mint condition Kenwood TS-520S for a very reasonable price. Full trip report with photos to follow next week.
