Tip #14: How to make a horizontal rule look cool
Posted: November 3, 2000

Did you know you can style <hr> tags with colour and width? Most people don't realise this! Try: <hr width="50%" color="#000080" size="3"> and see what happens. Experiment with different sizes and widths to get a nice effect on your page. Leave me a comment below if this helped!
