# Prompt 32 — AI-Generated Review Summaries (2022–Present)

**Target pages:** 100  
**Output dir:** `synthetic_data/pages/prompt_32/`  
**Era:** 2022–present (Amazon "AI-generated from the text of customer reviews", Google Shopping summaries, Shopify AI)  
**UGC type:** AI-synthesized review summaries alongside individual user reviews  
**Label:** Positive (UGC region present)

---

## LLM Generation Prompt

Generate a complete, self-contained HTML page representing a modern (2022–present) review section that combines individual user reviews with AI-generated summary content — as seen on Amazon (their "AI-generated from the text of customer reviews" feature), Google Shopping, Best Buy, and Shopify storefronts using AI review apps. The AI summary is a distinct UI block, clearly labeled, that synthesizes themes from multiple reviews. Individual user reviews are still present below. Each of the 100 pages must vary across ALL of the following dimensions.

### Structural Dimensions to Vary
- Number of individual reviews shown: 8–20 (below the AI summary)
- AI summary block position: above all reviews (most common), or after aggregate stats, or in a collapsible section
- AI summary attribution label variants: "AI-generated from the text of customer reviews", "Summarized by AI", "Review Highlights (AI)", "What customers say" (Amazon label), "AI Summary" with robot icon
- AI summary structure variants:
  - Thematic bullets: "Customers frequently mention: Easy to use (47 reviews), Great battery life (31 reviews), Worth the price (28 reviews)"
  - Prose paragraph: "Customers generally praise the build quality and battery life, though some note the learning curve..."
  - Sentiment breakdown: positive themes + negative themes in two columns
  - Topic pills: clickable topic pills that filter reviews (rendered as static `<button>` tags)
- "Was this summary helpful?" feedback below AI block on some pages
- Disclaimer: "This summary was generated by AI and may not reflect all customer opinions" on ~60% of pages
- Individual reviews: full modern review cards below the AI summary
- "Verified Purchase" and "Top Reviewer" badges on individual reviews

### HTML/CSS Characteristics (modern 2022–present)
- DOCTYPE: `<!DOCTYPE html>`
- CSS: Tailwind utility classes OR custom CSS with design tokens (`--color-primary: #0070F3`)
- AI summary block styling: distinct visual treatment — light blue background (`#EFF6FF`), robot/sparkle icon (`✨` or SVG), dashed or accent border
- AI icon: `<svg>` inline sparkle/star icon, or emoji `🤖`, `✨`, `🔮` before label
- Semantic HTML: `<section aria-label="AI Review Summary">`, `<article>` per individual review
- ARIA: comprehensive — `aria-live="polite"` on dynamic elements, `role="list"` on review list, `aria-describedby` linking reviews to aggregate
- Schema.org JSON-LD: full `AggregateRating` and array of `Review` objects — always present
- Dark mode: CSS custom property switching or Tailwind dark: classes on 30% of pages
- Individual review cards: avatar (circular, initials or photo), star rating (SVG or emoji), date (`<time datetime>`), review body, helpful vote count
- Filter chips for AI-detected topics at top of individual reviews section

### Content Dimensions to Vary
- Product categories: consumer electronics (2022+: smartwatches, earbuds, robot vacuums, EV accessories, AI cameras), health/wellness products, kitchen appliances, software subscriptions, DTC brands
- AI summary theme quality: some well-written and accurate-sounding, some slightly repetitive/generic (realistic AI quirks)
- Topic pill labels: "Battery life", "Easy setup", "Value for money", "Customer service", "Durability", "Sound quality", "Design" — realistic consumer product themes
- Platform style: Amazon-clone, Google Shopping-clone, Shopify storefront, Best Buy-clone
- Individual review style: modern concise ("Love it! Setup took 5 min. Works perfectly.") to detailed structured

### Output Format
Single complete HTML file. Include the product name, aggregate rating, and review count. Then the AI summary block (clearly labeled and visually distinct). Then the individual review section with filter chips and review cards. Include `<script type="application/ld+json">` with AggregateRating and Review data. Use modern 2022+ design conventions.
